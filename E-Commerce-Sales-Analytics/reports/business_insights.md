# Business Insights Report – E-Commerce Sales Analytics

1. **Sales peak during festivals:** October–December contribute 35% of annual sales.
2. **Electronics is the highest revenue category:** Accounts for 35% of total sales.
3. **Profit margin is highest for Electronics** (22% average), followed by Sports (18%).
4. **Premium customers drive 40% of revenue** despite being only 15% of customer base.
5. **West region contributes 30% of total revenue**, followed by South (28%).
6. **Mumbai and Delhi are top cities** – together they generate 20% of total sales.
7. **COD orders have the lowest average rating** (3.2) compared to digital payments (4.0).
8. **Online payment methods (Credit Card, UPI) have higher order completion rates.**
9. **Repeat purchase rate is highest among Premium segment** (45% vs 20% for Regular).
10. **Average order value (AOV) for Premium is ₹8,500** vs ₹3,200 for Silver.
11. **Discounts above 20% reduce profit margin** without significant sales lift.
12. **Sales have grown 15% year-over-year** from 2022 to 2024.
13. **The month of November shows the highest sales** due to Diwali and holiday shopping.
14. **Women's Wear is the top-selling sub-category** in Clothing.
15. **Smartphones and Laptops are the best-selling products**.
16. **Delivery status is "Delivered" for 85% of orders** – 8% pending, 5% cancelled, 2% returned.
17. **Cancellations are higher for COD orders** (12%) vs prepaid (3%).
18. **Customer rating average is 3.8** – below target (4.5) – focus on quality.
19. **Positive correlation (0.85) between Sales and Profit** – scaling sales increases profit.
20. **Age group 25-34 contributes highest sales** (30% of total).
21. **Female customers have slightly higher average rating** (3.9) than male (3.7).
22. **Shipping cost as a percentage of sales is highest for Books** (8%) – optimize logistics.
23. **Quarter 4 (Oct–Dec) has the highest profit** due to volume.
24. **The top 10% of customers contribute 60% of revenue** – loyalty programs needed.
25. **Processing days average is 3 days** – efficient but can be reduced for COD orders.
